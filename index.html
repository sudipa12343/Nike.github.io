What its Nike?

Nike, Inc. is a multinational corporation headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, USA. It was founded on January 25, 1964, by Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight under the name Blue Ribbon Sports, and officially became Nike, Inc. in 1971. Nike is known as one of the world's largest suppliers of athletic shoes, apparel, and sports equipment.

Nike boasts various product lines and renowned brands, including Air Jordan, Nike Golf, Converse, and Hurley International. The company is also famous for its innovative marketing campaigns and endorsement deals with prominent athletes such as Michael Jordan, LeBron James, Cristiano Ronaldo, and Serena Williams.
